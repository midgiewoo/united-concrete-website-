About Us

Over 30 years of concrete cutting excellence in Southern California

Our Story

United Concrete Coring & Sawing was started in 1993 with a single truck. From the very first client, the company built a solid reputation for professionalism, reliability, and on-time service.

One employee at a time, the team was trained in the United way of doing business. Today United operates a full fleet of diamond combo trucks, bobcat/dump truck teams, wall saw trucks, and jack hammer demolition crews — backed by a professional support staff to make sure every project is done on time, under budget, and done right.

Today

United Concrete is now owned and operated by Gabriel and Samantha Perches, who carry forward the same commitment to quality, safety, and customer service that has defined the company for over three decades.

United's trucks and equipment are state of the art and maintained with a very strict program. This ensures reliable equipment on every job, guaranteeing timely and productive work schedules. All trucks are fully self-contained with water and power, allowing us to complete your project no matter where the location.

From the moment we arrive on your job, our "Can Do" attitude speaks for itself. With over 30 years of experience in the concrete cutting industry, our goal is to make your project come in on time and under budget — from the smallest residential job to the largest industrial project.
